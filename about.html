About

Founded MindSumo in 2011 to help companies solve problems by crowdsourcing new ideas from students and recent grads. Grew it to 300,000+ registered users and nearly 100 Fortune 500 clients.

My wife and I started a full-time MBA back at Stanford in 2017.
